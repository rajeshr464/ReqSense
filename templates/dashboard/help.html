Troubleshooting

Upload Failure: "Invalid Format"

Ensure your file has a header row and is either a standard .CSV or .XLSX Excel workbook. Protected or encrypted files cannot be processed.

Excel Formatting Rule

For optimal mapping, your data must start at Cell A1. Avoid leaving empty rows or columns before your primary header row in the Excel sheet.

AI Response Latency

Large datasets (50k+ rows) may require additional time for neuro-metabolization. Please wait for the "Scanning" indicator to complete before issuing follow-up queries.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is "Neuro-Metabolization"?
Neuro-metabolization is our proprietary process of indexing your raw data into the Cerebras Inference Engine. This allows the AI to "understand" the relationships between columns and rows, enabling complex natural language queries and automated anomaly detection.
How do I delete a dataset safely?
In the Data Management screen, each node has a "Drop Node" action. Confirming this will permanently remove all architectural metadata and the source file from our Supabase vault. This action cannot be undone.
Can I use ReqSense on my mobile device?
Yes! ReqSense is fully responsive. The sidebar collapses into a mobile menu toggle, and all bento grids adjust to single-column layouts for high-density analysis on the go.
